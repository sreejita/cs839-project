 Crude oil for March delivery fell 1.8 percent to $56.93
today after trading began on the  New York  Mercantile Exchange.
The contract also lost 1.8 percent yesterday.  

 <markup>Norilsk</markup>, the world's largest producer of nickel, rose 0.9
